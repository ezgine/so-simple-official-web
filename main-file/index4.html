icon

健康 Health

報導指出，健康素食可有效降低血壓及膽固醇，減少患上心臟病、二型糖尿病及部分癌癥的機會。
Researches have showed that veggie diets may lower blood pressure and cholesterol level. It may also reduce the chances of suffering from heart disease, Type 2 diabetes and certain types of cancer.

icon

動物 Animal

保護動物並不是成為素食主義者的唯壹原因，但對許多人來說，這確實是他們決定吃素食的關鍵因素。
Preventing the exploitation of animals may not be the sole reason to become a vegan, but for most of them, this is a key factor which determined them to go veggie and stay veggie.

icon

環保 Environment

一個人可以為降低碳排放而所做的最有效方法之壹就是避免使用動物產品。
Avoid using animal products is one of the most effective ways to reduce one’s carbon footprint.

icon

矚目 Limelight

全球的素食飲食風氣在近年受到矚目，不僅有國際素食節、無肉日的節日推廣，在全球各地更可見素食或蔬食料理為主的餐廳紛紛開設。
In recent years, vegetarianism has stolen the limelight worldwide. Despite the promotion of International Vegetarian Week or Meat-free Days, vegetarian restaurants have gained popularity all over the world.

icon

趨勢 Trend

素食不僅是個人采用的飲食方式，當以國家甚至是全球的素食人口來看待這個飲食模式，素食便成為壹種強有力的飲食趨勢。
Vegetarianism is not only an individual’s choice of eating habit; when we look at the vegetarian population globally, it provides solid evidence that Vegetarianism has become an important diet trend.

icon

發展 Development

這股飲食風潮也影響食品產業及周邊產業的發展。
The important diet trend greatly affects the development of the food processing and related industries.

01

中環 Central

我們將於首階段開設 So Simple 實體零售點，首選地點是香港中環，以建立品牌形象及客戶消費信心。 實體零售點也是網上眾籌計劃的強力後盾，讓支持者可親身體驗我們的出品，再決定支持這個項目。
In the ﬁrst phase, we will launch via a physical So Simple retail outlet, preliminarily in Central, to establish brand image and build consumer conﬁdence. The So Simple retail outlet provides the major evidence to online crowdfunding, supporters may try out the products to decide whether they are interested to support.

02

最後一棒 "Last Mile" Solution

要為素食者傳最後壹棒，外送服務絕不可少
Delivery service is the crucial element to provide the ultimate solution to vegetarians

第一階段：香港中環商業區 Phase 1:Central Business Area, Hong Kong
第二階段：港島中西區 Phase 2:Central and Western Districts
第三階段：觀塘商業區 Phase 3:Kwun Tong Business Area

03

自助售賣機 Vending Machin

為解決素食者的多項痛點，以及為他們傳最後一棒。我們將鋪設智能自助售賣機，第壹階段目標在香港中環鋪設100臺，針對中環商圈的龐大需求。
To eliminate the pain Vegetarians are experiencing, and to be the reliable solution, we will implement vending machines. Initially, we will target at the growing demand in Central, Hong Kong and we aim to set up 100 vending machines.

最後一棒：將智能售賣機鋪設在目前人群的500公尺步行距離之內，為他們創造簡單輕松的購買體驗。
會員預訂：透過智能流動應用程式，會員可預留售賣機中的餐盒，以免向隅。

04

流動應用程式 Mobile Application

透過只能流動應用程式，客戶可以查看售賣機的位置及存貨狀況。更可加入會員，以更優惠價錢及智能預訂 So Simple 智能餐盒。日後更會在應用程式中加入智能健康管理功能。
Users may check the location and stock availability of the nearby vending machines via the mobile app. They may also receive discounts and pre-order So Simple lunchboxes by becoming a member. In the next step, we will add intelligent health management function in the mobile app.
